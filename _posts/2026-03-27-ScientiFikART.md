We're doing something a little different this time — welcome to **ScientiFikART**! 🎨

Instead of our usual Friday science format, we're hosting a **Science & Art showcase**. Bring a piece that connects — loosely or tightly — to science, research, nature, tech, data, the academic life, or simply the way you see the world.

**How it works:** each participant presents their piece for a few minutes, and the audience votes for their favorite at the end. Most presentations will receive a prize! 🏆 All levels of artistic experience are welcome — this is about expression, not expertise.

The event is open to everyone. 🌍

**📅 Deadline to submit: April 15th** — <a href="https://forms.gle/2Mofs5C4ofuSwFar8" target="_blank">Sign up here!</a>

*Participants may be featured on our website and Instagram.* 📸
